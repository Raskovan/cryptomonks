The Team

The MettaYogis project is build with the aspiration to beneift all. The exterienced team is based in France and has a solid history with Buddhism, Crypto and Art.

Karma Trinlay Rinpoche

He is a highly accomplished Buddhist teacher and meditation master. Rinpoche is trilingual with equal competence in English, French and Tibetan. A charismatic speaker, Karma Trinlay Rinpoche is respected for his lucid mind and diligence in both study and practice. People widely appreciate his inner calmness, sweet disposition, and sharp mind. He has taught in Buddhist centers and universities throughout the world.

Ira

She is a trained artist with experience in painting tradiitonal Buddhist artwork. Living in France she performs commissioned works for monasteries throughout the French countryside.

Sylvestre

As a trained illustrator, Sylvestre works closely with artists such as Ira to bring the traitional works into the modern era of digital illustration.

Richard

He is an entrepreneur and software developer living in Paris, France, and was originally raised on a dairy farm in the middle of California. He discovered a strong interest in software development at an early age.

About the Mahamati Foundation

Backing the MettaYogis project is the Mahamati Foundation, a 501(c)-3 non-profit organization.

The mission of the foundation is to promote the activities and teachings of the Karma Kagyu tradition of Tibetan Buddhism under the ultimate direction of the 17th Karmapa, Thaye Dorje.
