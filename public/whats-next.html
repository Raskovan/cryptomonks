Is it a fair mint?

A Yogi is a person who strives to discover his innate qualities. Every Metta Yogi is motivated by Metta (word in pali for loving-kindness), the sincere wish that all beings have happiness and peace.

About the Mahamati Foundation

Backing the MettaYogis project is the Mahamati Foundation, a 501(c)-3 non-profit organization.

The mission of the foundation is to promote the activities and teachings of the Karma Kagyu tradition of Tibetan Buddhism under the ultimate direction of the 17th Karmapa, Thaye Dorje, through the specific activities of Karma Trinlay Rinpoche, that include his extensive teachings on various important Buddhist texts and practices, his writings and translations, as well as his organizing of seminars and retreats.
